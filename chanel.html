N~5 COLLECTION

THE 55.55 NECKLACE

In 1921, Gabrielle Chanel launched a fragrance she called N°5—a moniker inspired by her lucky number and an idea that would make her a legend. To mark one hundred years of the N°5 perfume, CHANEL High Jewelry created the N°5 Collection, which is crowned by a necklace whose design reflects the defining features of the N°5 perfume bottle. More than 700 diamonds compose the necklace and surround a D Flawless 55.55-carat diamond—the custom weight gives the diamond the signature of CHANEL.

COLLECTION

In 1932, Gabrielle Chanel introduces her first and only jewelry collection, Bijoux de Diamants, breaking with the codes of traditional jewelry. The famous star, the stones fitting the design of the jewel, the word “collection” borrowed from fashion or the use of wax figures for a natural display of the jewels. This vision of jewelry, this audacity and this unique creativity are still as present today.
